No Bake Cookies

Description

What's better than baking? Not having to bake! With these delicious no bake cookies, you'll never have to bake a day in your life but you'll still get the opportunity to share a yummy cookie with your loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups white sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup crunchy peanut butter
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • waxed paper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine sugar, butter, milk, and cocoa in a medium saucepan; bring to a boil, stirring often. Boil for exactly 1 ½ minutes.
  3. Remove from heat, and stir in peanut butter.
  4. Add oats and vanilla; stir until well combined.
  5. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper.
  6. Let cool until hardened. Enjoy!
